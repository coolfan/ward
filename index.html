Have you ever struggled with room draw? Been lost in a labarynth like room draw guide, full of the pitfalls of outdated information? Then wait no longer! With WARD you can access the wisdom of those who came before you through reviews and a comprehensive draw guide, along with likelihood predictors to help you figure out if you will be able to select a room. Choose WARD - room DRAW service at its best.

Check out our site at rooms.cs.princeton.edu

The 5 course instructors were given fake room draw times and all put in the same draw group, so you should be able to access all the features of our website simply by logging in through CAS.
